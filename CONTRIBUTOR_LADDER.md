# KubeOrch Contributor Ladder

This document outlines the roles and responsibilities within the KubeOrch community, and the path for contributors to grow their involvement.

For maintainer selection and governance processes, see [GOVERNANCE.md](https://github.com/KubeOrch/.github/blob/main/GOVERNANCE.md).

## Roles

### 1. Contributor

A Contributor is anyone who contributes to the project in any form.

**How to become a Contributor:**
- Open an issue or pull request
- Participate in discussions
- Improve documentation
- Report bugs or suggest features
- Help other users

**Responsibilities:**
- Follow the [Code of Conduct](https://github.com/KubeOrch/.github/blob/main/CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md)
- Follow the [Contributing Guide](https://github.com/KubeOrch/.github/blob/main/CONTRIBUTING.md)
- Sign commits with DCO (Developer Certificate of Origin)

**Privileges:**
- Listed in GitHub contributors
- May be invited to the KubeOrch GitHub organization

---

### 2. Reviewer

A Reviewer has demonstrated regular, quality contributions and domain expertise. Reviewers help maintain code quality by reviewing pull requests.

**Requirements:**
- Active contributor for at least 3 months
- At least 5 merged pull requests
- Demonstrated understanding of the codebase in their area of expertise
- Consistently helpful and constructive in reviews and discussions

**How to become a Reviewer:**
- Nominated by an existing Maintainer or Approver
- Approved by simple majority of Maintainers

**Responsibilities:**
- Review pull requests in their area of expertise
- Provide timely, constructive feedback
- Help contributors improve their submissions
- Triage issues in their domain

**Privileges:**
- Listed as a Reviewer in MAINTAINERS.md
- `@kubeorch/reviewers` team membership
- Can approve PRs (approval required but not sufficient for merge)

---

### 3. Approver

An Approver is a trusted Reviewer who has demonstrated leadership and deep knowledge of the project. Approvers can approve pull requests for merging.

**Requirements:**
- Active Reviewer for at least 3 months
- Demonstrated leadership and mentorship
- Deep understanding of project architecture and design principles
- Track record of high-quality reviews

**How to become an Approver:**
- Nominated by an existing Maintainer
- Approved by simple majority of Maintainers

**Responsibilities:**
- All Reviewer responsibilities
- Approve pull requests for merge
- Ensure changes align with project direction and architecture
- Mentor Reviewers and Contributors

**Privileges:**
- Listed as an Approver in MAINTAINERS.md
- `@kubeorch/approvers` team membership
- Can approve and merge PRs

---

### 4. Maintainer

Maintainers are the stewards of the project. They set the project direction, manage releases, and make governance decisions.

**Requirements:**
- 6+ months of active participation
- Demonstrated leadership in one or more areas: active reviewing and approving, component leadership (frontend, backend, security), mentoring contributors, or community building
- Understanding of project policies and processes
- Willingness to take on additional maintainer duties

**How to become a Maintainer:**
- Nominated by an existing Maintainer via PR to MAINTAINERS.md
- Approved by simple majority vote of existing Maintainers
- See [GOVERNANCE.md](https://github.com/KubeOrch/.github/blob/main/GOVERNANCE.md) for full details

**Responsibilities:**
- All Approver responsibilities
- Set project direction and strategy
- Manage releases
- Participate in governance decisions and voting
- Attend community meetings
- Respond to security reports

**Privileges:**
- Listed as a Maintainer in MAINTAINERS.md
- Full repository admin access
- Voting rights on governance decisions
- Represent the project publicly

---

## Stepping Down and Emeritus Status

Contributors at any level may step down voluntarily at any time. Maintainers who step down are moved to Emeritus status in MAINTAINERS.md and may be reinstated by majority vote if they wish to return.

Maintainers who are inactive for 6+ months may be moved to Emeritus status. See [GOVERNANCE.md](https://github.com/KubeOrch/.github/blob/main/GOVERNANCE.md) for the full process.
